Major Meditation

cused attention meditation (FAM), is a mindfulness practice where you concentrate on a single point, like your breath, a mantra, or a physical sensation, to train your mind to stay present and improve focus

Automatic self-transcending (AST) meditation is a type of meditation characterized by its effortless nature and ability to induce a state of transcendence with minimal cognitive control

Open monitoring (OM) meditation is a practice that involves observing the present moment without judgment, focusing on the continuous flow of thoughts, sensations, and emotions as they arise and pass away
